Gaming

So maybe you're tired of watching YouTube or scrolh2ng through TikTok, and you're asking yourself: What should I do now? Wow, I'm so bored! There's nothing to do. Well whether you're using a pc, phone, tablet, etc., you can download games onto these devices. So what games should you download and play???


Here's a list of free and paid games you can try out!

Free Games

Subway Surfers

You ride the subway, and you probably hate it. Well now you can hop on top of subways with this game. It's offline endless runner, single player mobile game. The goal is to outrun a train inspector while collecting coins and avoiding obstacles.

Team Fortress 2

You like chaos and first-person shooters? Here's a multiplayer first-person shooter game that features different classes for players to play with.

League of Legends

A 5v5 multiplayer team-based strategy game where two teams face each other to see who can destroy the other team's nexus first. You can play with over 100+ champions ranging from random animals to mechanical humans.

Clash Royale

You like card games and RTS games? Well here's a game that combines those genres with a tower rush mode.

Skrribl.io

Want to play pictionary but online with strangers. Just type skrribl.io on your browser to play pictionary with friends and strangers around the world.

Do you like bugs? Well this game is all about a bug kingdom that has been abandon and is infected by a mysterious plague. A Metroidvania game where you can gain new abih2ties as you explore and fight bosses to test your skills.

Hate cities? Well me too. Well now you can escape your city h2fe with this game about farming, mining, and being a good farmer in a town full of NPCs that you can befriend. There's also an option to make some of them be your girlfriend/boyfriend and eventually get married!

Do you play RPGS? Have you ever played a visual novel esque game? Well, here's a game that combines those two genres with a hand drawn style art and a heartfelt story. However, be warned as it's a game that contains deep themes h2ke depression, anxiety, suicide, etc.

Minecraft

Do you h2ke building and blocks? Well Minecraft is a sandbox type experience that offers game modes h2ke creative where you can build in peace and survival where it's you against the world.

Any Pokémon Game

So you like RPGs and you want to have pets that fight for you. Every Pokémon game features a world brimming with animals called Pokémon that you can call your friends to fight against enemy trainers. The ultimate goal is to become the Pokémon champion.

Henry Stickmin Collection

You just have to play this game. It's a collection of the 5 previously free flash games plus a 6th final game. It's a point and ch2ck style game with various paths and endings.
